NEET Tips & Strategy

Proven tips from toppers to crack NEET

01

NCERT First, Always

80%+ NEET questions come directly from NCERT. Read every line carefully, especially Biology diagrams and definitions.

02

Biology = Top Priority

Biology has 360 marks out of 720. Master it and you're halfway to a great score. Don't neglect Zoology.

03

Solve PYQ Daily

Previous year questions repeat in NEET. Solve at least 20 PYQs daily — it improves speed and accuracy both.

04

Revision > New Topics

In last 2 months, stop reading new topics. Revise what you know 3-4 times. Memory retention is the key.

05

Mock Tests Every Week

Give full 3.5 hour mock test every Sunday. Analyze mistakes the next day. Improvement comes from review, not just practice.

06

Sleep & Health Matter

7-8 hours sleep is mandatory. A tired brain can't retain. Study smart — 6 focused hours beats 12 distracted hours.
